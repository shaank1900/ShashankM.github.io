Welcome to my personal website!

I am currently a PhD candidate at the Department of Econometrics and Operations Research at Tilburg University. I am supervised by Dr. Marike Knoef, Dr. Jorgo Goossens and Dr. Eduard Ponds.

My interests lie in using applied econometric methods to mainly answer questions in the fields of Health Economics, Retirement & Savings and Development Economics.

I primarily use large-scale survey data and administrative data in my research.
